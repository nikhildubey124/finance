üöÄ Quick Start Guide

Welcome to FinanceTracker! Follow these simple steps to get started in minutes.

Step 1: Create Your Account

  1. Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage
  2. Fill in your details (name, email, username, password)
  3. Click "Register" to create your account
  4. You'll be redirected to the login page

Step 2: Login to Your Account

  1. Enter your username and password
  2. Click "Login"
  3. You'll be taken to your personalized dashboard

Step 3: Add Your First Transaction

  1. Click "Add Transaction" in the navigation menu
  2. Select transaction type (Credit for income, Debit for expense)
  3. Enter the amount, date, and category
  4. Add an optional description
  5. Click "Add Transaction" to save
üí° Pro Tip: Start by adding your most recent transactions to see your current financial snapshot immediately.

üìä Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is the central hub where you can view all your financial information at a glance. It displays:

  • Total Balance: Your current financial position (Income - Expenses)
  • Total Income: Sum of all credit transactions
  • Total Expenses: Sum of all debit transactions
  • Monthly Trends: Visual charts showing spending patterns
  • Category Breakdown: Pie charts of expenses by category
  • Recent Transactions: Your latest financial activities
  • Budget Status: Progress on your monthly budgets

üí≥ Managing Transactions

Adding Transactions

Record every income and expense to maintain accurate financial records.

  1. Navigate to "Add Transaction"
  2. Choose transaction type:
    • Credit: For income (salary, refunds, gifts)
    • Debit: For expenses (groceries, bills, entertainment)
  3. Enter the amount (numeric value only)
  4. Select the transaction date
  5. Choose a category from the dropdown
  6. Add a description (optional but recommended)
  7. Click "Add Transaction"

Viewing Transaction History

Access your complete transaction history with advanced filtering options:

  • Filter by Type: View only income or expense transactions
  • Filter by Category: See transactions for specific categories
  • Filter by Date: Select date ranges to analyze specific periods
  • Search: Find transactions by description or amount

Editing and Deleting Transactions

Made a mistake? No problem! You can easily edit or delete transactions:

  1. Go to "Transactions" page
  2. Find the transaction you want to modify
  3. Click "Edit" to update details or "Delete" to remove
  4. Confirm your changes

üè∑Ô∏è Category Management

Categories help you organize your transactions and understand your spending patterns. FinanceTracker comes with built-in system categories and allows you to create custom ones.

System Categories

Pre-configured categories that cannot be edited or deleted:

  • Income: Salary, Business Income, Investment Returns, Gifts
  • Expenses: Food, Transportation, Housing, Entertainment, Healthcare, Education, etc.

Creating Custom Categories

  1. Navigate to "Categories"
  2. Click "+ Add Custom Category"
  3. Enter category name
  4. Select type (Credit or Debit)
  5. Choose an emoji icon (optional)
  6. Pick a color for visual identification (optional)
  7. Select a parent category to create subcategories (optional)
  8. Click "Create Category"
üí° Example: Create a "Freelance" category under "Business Income" to track freelance earnings separately.

üéØ Budget Tracking

Set monthly budgets for different categories to control spending and achieve financial goals.

Creating a Budget

  1. Go to "Budgets"
  2. Click "Add Budget"
  3. Select the category you want to budget
  4. Enter the budget amount
  5. Select the month (defaults to current month)
  6. Click "Create Budget"

Monitoring Budget Progress

Track how much you've spent against your budget limits:

  • Green: Under budget - you're doing great!
  • Yellow: Approaching limit - be careful
  • Red: Over budget - time to cut back

üìÅ Bulk Upload

Import historical transactions quickly using Excel or CSV files. Perfect for migrating from other systems or adding past month data.

Step 1: Download Template

  1. Navigate to "Bulk Upload"
  2. Choose your preferred format:
    • Excel (.xlsx): Recommended for most users
    • CSV (.csv): For advanced users or compatibility
  3. Click "Download Template"

Step 2: Fill in Your Data

Open the template and add your transactions with these columns:

  • Date: Format YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 2026-01-15)
  • Type: Either "CREDIT" or "DEBIT"
  • Category: Exact category name (case-insensitive)
  • Amount: Numeric value (e.g., 150.50)
  • Description: Optional details about the transaction
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: All dates must be from the previous month. Current month transactions should be added manually.

Step 3: Upload Your File

  1. Drag and drop your file or click to browse
  2. Wait for validation to complete
  3. Review any errors and fix them in your file
  4. Re-upload if needed
  5. Once successful, transactions will be imported

Export Transactions

  4. Click "Export"
  5. Download the CSV file for analysis in Excel or other tools

üîß Troubleshooting

Cannot Login

  • Verify your username and password are correct
  • Ensure Caps Lock is off
  • Try the "Forgot Password" feature
  • Clear your browser cache and try again

Bulk Upload Errors

  • "Date must be from previous month": Check date format and ensure dates are from last month
  • "Category not found": Verify category names match exactly (spelling matters)
  • "Invalid amount": Use numeric values only, no currency symbols
  • "Duplicate transaction": Transaction with same date, amount, and category already exists

Missing Transactions

  • Check your filters - you might have filtered out the transactions
  • Verify the date range selected
  • Ensure you're logged in with the correct account

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is my financial data secure?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption to protect your personal information. All sensitive data including your name, email, phone number, and username are encrypted using industry-standard AES encryption. Your password is also hashed using bcrypt for maximum security.

Can I use FinanceTracker on my phone?

Absolutely! FinanceTracker is fully responsive and works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Just access it through your mobile browser.

How do I change my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with a secure reset link valid for 30 minutes.

Can I delete my account?

Please contact support for account deletion requests. We'll process your request promptly and ensure all your data is permanently removed from our systems.

What file formats are supported for bulk upload?

We support Excel (.xlsx, .xls) and CSV (.csv) files. Excel format is recommended as it includes helpful formatting and instructions.
